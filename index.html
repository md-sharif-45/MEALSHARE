What We Do & Why We Do

In our community, we face a dual challenge: food waste and hunger:

Food Waste:
Each day, a significant amount of food goes to waste, contributing to environmental issues and resource depletion.
Hunger:
Simultaneously, many in our community struggle with food insecurity, lacking access to nutritious meals.

MEALSHARE is born out of a commitment to bridge this gap. Our platform is designed to connect surplus food donors with those in need, creating a seamless and efficient way to redistribute excess resources within our community.
